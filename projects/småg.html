Småg

Småg is a character me and my girlfriend made in 2021. It resembles some sort of a bird with wiggly legs and vibrant colors. Somehow, the Småg became very popular among the first year students that started studying at LBS in the fall of 2022. The Småg is now part of the school's lore. We also made a sheet with step-by-step instructions on how to structure a basic Småg. As we know, they come in many forms.
