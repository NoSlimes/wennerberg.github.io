Stunning Disco

Stunning Disco is a game project we made as a group project durin "Digitalt Skapande 2", "digital creating 2" class. In the game 2 players control characters and compete against each other in finishing key combinations. It's a little bit similar to Friday Night Funkin'. If we'd gotten that far, the player who was in the lead would have pushed the crowd surrounding the platforms the players were standing on more towards the other player, eventually throwing them off their platform.
